08 / FAQ

STRAIGHT ANSWERS

How much will my job cost?

Flat-rate by load size: minimum $150, ¼ trailer $175, ½ trailer $275, ¾ trailer $350, full trailer $425. Labor, hauling, dump fees and sweep-out are all included. Text a photo to (208) 530-5009 and we'll send a flat price in under an hour.

How fast can you come out?

Same-day service is available across Rigby, Idaho Falls and Ammon — most jobs are cleared within hours. Call or text and we'll give you the earliest slot.

What's the catch with the pricing?

No catch. Your flat price is locked before we lift a finger. The only extras are clearly listed add-ons (like a fridge +$45 or stairs +$50) and travel beyond our home zone — and we tell you all of it up front.

Do you charge a travel fee?

Rigby and Menan are free. Idaho Falls, Ammon, Rexburg, Sugar City and Roberts add $25. St. Anthony, Blackfoot and Pocatello add $50. That's it.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed and insured. Your property is protected the whole time we're on the job.

Do I need to be there?

Often no. If you can arrange access and we've agreed on the flat price, we can knock it out and send you before/after photos when we're done. Perfect for landlords and property managers.
